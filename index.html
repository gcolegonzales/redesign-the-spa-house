Our Story

A calm corner of Gonzales

The Spa House Day Spa is a locally owned retreat on Highway 621, built around one simple idea: you deserve to be genuinely cared for. From the moment you arrive, our experienced staff guide you into a world of complete calm.

We offer the very best in specialized skincare — customized facials, microdermabrasion, and chemical peels — alongside massage therapy tailored to you, from Swedish and deep-tissue to prenatal and hot-stone. We also specialize in permanent makeup, body treatments, and face & body waxing.

Every one of our massage therapists, estheticians, and permanent-makeup artists is licensed and experienced — and dedicated to exceeding your expectations.
